Corgi

Vital Stats:

  • Dog Breed Group: Herding Dogs
  • Height: 10 inches to 1 foot tall at the shoulder
  • Weight: Up to 30 pounds
  • Life Span: 12 to 14 years

Although Pembrokes are still used as working dogs, they are most often seen as family pets these days. They are known for being happy, loving, and intelligent, but with a stubborn or independent streak at times.
